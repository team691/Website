Blue Bot

Challenge: Lunacy
Year: 2009
How we addressed the challenge: Blue Bot has a four wheel design. it picks up balls from the floor with ease due to it's conveyor belt. it drops these balls off at the shooter for a quick shot into the opposing team's trailers. Also, it's heavy weight made it a bully on the field.
Unique robot features: • 4 wheel drive
• able to hold many balls before shooting them at high speed
Results: Blue Bot made it into the play-offs, but was defeated in the first round.
