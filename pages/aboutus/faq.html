Frequently Asked Questions

What is FIRST? FIRST stands for For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology. It is an organization that uses a robotics competition in order to furtheran interest in science and technology in today's youth in America and around the world. more information about FIRST and their mission can be found on their website.
What does Project 691 have to do with first? Project 691 is a team of high school team that participates in the annual FIRST Robotics Competition challenge.
